How to Play

Point of the Game

  • Survive as long as possible while eliminating enemies
  • Scoring: Total seconds survived + number of kills

Game Interface

  • Stamina Bar (Top-Left): Manage your energy - hold SHIFT to boost speed
  • Health Bar: Below stamina - watch this carefully!
  • Ammo Counter (XX/XXX): Current clip / reserve ammo (Max 180)
  • Low Ammo: Golden lines guide you to nearest ammo kit when you have no bullets
  • Low Health: Red lines guide you to nearest medical kit when you have at max 40 hp
  • Collectibles: Bullet icons for ammo, Red kits for health

Controls

  • Movement: W, A, S, D keys
  • Turret Rotation: <> Arrow keys
  • Actions: R (Reload), SPACE (Shoot)
  • Special: L (Laser sight), SHIFT (Speed boost)
  • Mines: Press 2 to place mine that will explode after 3 seconds and damage all enemies within the radius (you have only 2 mines for the entire game)
  • Shooting Enemies: They will appear after first 15 seconds

Boss

  • The boss will spawn every minute
  • Boss can shoot 8 bullets in all directions with spiral trajectory
  • Boss can also spawn new enemies and plants on which if you stand, you will receive damage

Cheats

  • H - Set invulnerability (for testing purposes)
